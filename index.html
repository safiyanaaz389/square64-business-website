  • About us

    Square 64 Capital Advisors LLP  offers Portfolio Management Services, based
    out of  Mumbai. Square 64 has been ideated on the concept of ‘Value Investing’.
    It follows the simple strategy of investing in the right publicly listed companies,
    and then holding tight till the markets play out and the stock achieves the
    desired results.

     

    The firm has three partners at its helm – Priyankar Sarkar, Ankit Poddar and
    Vishwanshu Agarwal. Each of the partners has more than a decade of
    experience in portfolio management, business development and operations
    management. 

    Why 'Square 64'?

    The firm’s name comes from the famous Indian folktale of ‘The King and the Rice’.
    You may well be aware of this story, wherein a farmer beats a King at a game of
    chess. As a reward, he asks for one grain of rice on the first square of the
    chessboard, two grains on the second, four grains on the third, and so on,
    doubling the number of grains for each square. While this seems like a small ask,
    by the 64th square, the King would owe him 18 quintillion grains of rice – that is
    more rice than has ever been cultivated on earth.
    That is the power of compounding! 

    Square 64 endeavours to achieve returns on the 64th square. With this clear
    objective of gaining non-linear returns, there are certain investment principles
    that form the foundation of Square 64’s investment philosophy.
